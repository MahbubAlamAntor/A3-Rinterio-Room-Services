Functionality

Determine how each room will be used. For example, do you need a workspace in your living room, or is the bedroom purely for relaxation? Furniture should support the activities you plan to do in each space.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle. If you have pets or young children, you might want to choose durable, stain-resistant materials.

Room Size

Measure each room to understand what will fit. Take into account doorways, windows, and how much space you need to move around comfortably.

Furniture Size

Ensure the furniture is proportionate to the room size. Avoid overfilling a space with large pieces, or making it feel empty with too-small furniture.
